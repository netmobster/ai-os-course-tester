THE PROBLEM

Why Your AI Sessions Feel Like Starting Over Every Time.

You've probably noticed a pattern. You open a new chat, explain your context, get a useful response — and then the next day you start from scratch. Or you paste the same background information into every session because the AI "forgot." Or you get answers that are technically correct but don't account for how you actually work, what you've already tried, or what matters in your specific situation.

Most people respond to this by trying to write better prompts. Longer, more detailed, more specific. And that helps — a little, for a session. But it doesn't solve the underlying problem, which isn't the prompts.

The problem is that the AI doesn't know you. Every session, it meets you for the first time. You're not getting a bad AI — you're getting a capable AI operating on almost no information about the person it's supposed to help. The output quality ceiling isn't the model. It's the relationship.

The ceiling isn't the model. It's the relationship.

A capable AI working from a detailed model of who you are and how you operate will outperform a more capable AI working from a blank slate. Every time. This course builds the model.

WHAT THIS IS

An Operating System, Not a Prompt Library.

This course teaches you to build an AI OS — a lightweight system that installs in any AI tool, travels with you across platforms and employers, and compounds over time instead of resetting with each session.

It's not a collection of prompts to copy. It's not a workflow template. It's a structured approach to giving an AI a working model of you — how you process information, how you make decisions, what your failure modes are, and what you need from an AI partner to do your best work.

Once built, it lives in a text file. You paste it into Claude Projects, ChatGPT Custom Instructions, or any AI that accepts a system prompt. Every session starts from an oriented position instead of zero. The relationship builds instead of restarting.

WHAT YOU'LL BUILD

SIX INSTALLABLE ARTIFACTS — ONE PER UNIT

Each unit delivers one piece of the system. By Unit 6, you have a complete AI OS installed and running. Unit 7 is a free bonus — what comes after, and where to take it next.

01
Your Self-Profile
Five sentences. How you actually process information, make decisions, and what your failure mode is. The AI reads this before everything else. The difference between a response shaped for you and a response shaped for anyone.
02
Your Operator Profile
The things that would take a new colleague weeks to figure out about you — compressed into a paragraph. Career context, working style, current mission. Orientation instead of guessing.
03
Your Behavioral Contract
The constraints that make the AI useful instead of pleasant. Three rules that give it explicit permission to push back, stay direct, and not do the things that make AI responses feel like AI responses.
04
Your Gear System
Trigger phrases that shift the AI's posture on demand. Interview mode. Handoff mode. Whatever your work actually requires. One AI, multiple operating modes — without switching tools.
05
Your Session Ritual
An Opening Protocol and Closing Protocol that take 90 seconds each and turn isolated sessions into a compounding sequence. The state document you produce at close is what makes the next session smarter than this one.
06
Your Complete AI OS
All five layers assembled in one document, installable anywhere. You'll know how to move it to a new tool in five minutes and upgrade it when a new model ships without losing what you built.
